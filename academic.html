Research Interests

My primary research focuses on the physics of Bose-Einstein Condensates (BEC) and ultracold atom systems. I am particularly interested in:

  • Time-of-flight imaging analysis and diagnostics
  • Optical lattices and trap dynamics
  • Quantum simulation using neutral atoms

Current Projects

Bimodal Distribution Analysis

Developing Python-based computational tools to analyze experimental data from atom clouds. The project involves fitting bimodal distributions to separate the thermal cloud fraction from the condensate fraction in time-of-flight images.

Laser Cooling Simulation

Simulating the interaction between Gaussian laser beams and atomic ensembles to optimize evaporative cooling sequences.
